Utilitarian Approach

Maximize total utility: Prioritize based on who has the highest risk of death or who is most essential to society's functioning. Goal: Maximize the total number of quality years of life saved.

References: Bentham, Mill, Singer

Kantian Approach

Fair distribution: Prioritize based on fairness or duty, making sure no person is treated as a means to the greater good. Methods: Lottery or highest vulnerability regardless of social utility.

References: Kant, O'Neill
